About Us

Our goal is to make it easy for students at the University of Florida to create potential schedules for their semester.

We have many ideas for upcoming features, which include:

  • Recommends courses to keep you on track to graduate.
  • See if your friends are in particular classes.
  • Easily share your schedules via facebook, and see what your friends are taking!
