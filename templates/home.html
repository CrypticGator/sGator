Welcome to ScheduleGator!

ScheduleGator is a web application built for University of Florida students. With ScheduleGator you can generate a schedule tailored to your lifestyle! Courses will be made selected based on professor rating and average GPA while accommodating your timing preferences and keeping you on track to graduate. ScheduleGator is currently in development and will be released soon.

Upcoming Features To Look Forward To

  • Automatically generate your course schedule
  • Choose your timing preferences to avoid early morning and late night classes
  • Selects professors based on rating so you can have the best instructors UF has to offer
  • Recommends courses to keep you on track to graduate.
  • Share your schedule and see what friends are taking with Facebook integration
