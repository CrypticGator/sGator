Welcome to ScheduleGator!

ScheduleGator is a web application built for University of Florida students. With ScheduleGator you can generate a schedule tailored to your lifestyle! Courses are selected based on professor rating and average GPA while accommodating your timing preferences and keeping you on track to graduate. ScheduleGator is now in open beta!

Right now you can:

  • Browse courses using our clean user interface, and then add them to the schedule creator.
  • Automatically generate your course schedule
  • Choose your timing preferences to avoid early morning and late night classes
  • Define a credit range
  • Choose your classes based on professor ratings, which are displayed with the courses. You don't even have to leave our site!
